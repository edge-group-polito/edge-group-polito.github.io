The EDGE Group is a research group at the VLSI Lab, Dipartimento di Elettronica e
Telecomunicazioni (DET), Politecnico di Torino, working on energy-efficient embedded
systems, RISC-V platforms and hardware security.

The group designs custom RISC-V cores and SoC platforms, hardware accelerators for
cryptography — including post-quantum schemes — and for AI/edge inference, and the EDA
flows needed to bring them to silicon and FPGA. Recent projects include CHIMERA and
HORCRUX (cryptographic accelerators), LEN5 (an out-of-order RISC-V core) and
Mage-and-Rogue (a CGRA for AI acceleration); the [Projects](/projects/) page tracks the
full, live list.

The group is active at RISC-V Summits and similar venues, and collaborates with other
academic labs and industry partners.
