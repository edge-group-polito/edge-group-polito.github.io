Delete this file once there is a real article — it exists to show the format.

Copy it to `_posts/YYYY-MM-DD-some-slug.md`, keep the front matter above, and write the
paper's abstract as Markdown below. It appears as a box on `/articles/` and in
"Latest articles" on the landing page automatically. The date in the filename must
match the `date:` field, and a future date will not be published.

`authors` and `citation` are shown under the title on the box; this body text is the
abstract, hidden until the box is clicked to expand. `paper_url` and `repo_url` are
both optional and each add a link on the right of the box — drop `repo_url` entirely
when a paper has no accompanying repository.
